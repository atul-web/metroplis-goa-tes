Hotel Metropolis, Goa

Best Hotel in Goa

Goa has a vast stretch of beaches, a blend of Portuguese and Indian culture, temples, coconut grooves, and many more beautiful things to be explored. Metropolis Business hotel has its roots in the heart of Goa. It is closely accessible to markets, shopping points, international stores, and tantalizing food spots.

Breathtaking ambiance, luxurious rooms, and top-notch services are our prime motto. It is literally a home away from home with the latest amenities and technologies depicting the Goan culture, and dedicated service teams providing 24/7 assistance. It is strategically located in the center of famous casinos on River Mandovi giving the best prospects to explore Goa, what could one ask more for?

Smell the earth, taste the coconuts, have sight of the trees, and enhance the feeling of being alive. After everything, rest at Metropolis Business Hotel, nothing less than a paradise on earth. Be our guest, as it is a luxury worth experiencing. We serve finger-licking food and you’ll never know it until you try it. An affair to be experienced as there are specially designed rooms for everyone and food that will get your taste buds happy all the time. We have designed everything for a leisure stay or a corporate meet for everyone.
